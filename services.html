Pre-show:
We come to your house or playground of your choice 15 minutes prior to the show, change into character based on your kids wishes, set up the professional audio system with kids-friendly music and get everyone excited for the big event. All shows usually involve 2 members of our staff.

Show:
Your childs favourite characters start the interactive show with an introduction in which we congratulate the child and give them a gift. Magic begins as we start off with interactive activities EX: WORLD'S BIGGEST BIRTHDAY CARD!!! then we move on to team challenges like egg race, tunnel race, treasure hunt and way more. We end the celebration with pictures, and the loudest birthday song of all. Keep in mind you are free to customize the performance the way you see fit. YOUR WISH IS OUR COMMAND.

Themes & Characters (all costumes we have and are free of charge):
Superheroes (Spider Man, Batman, Avengers), Comical Clowns, Pirates, Princes, Elfs, Knights, Baby Shark Mascot and Disney. Contact us to find out more.
